CREATE A TRIGGER FOR IF MARKS INSERTION HAVE A WRONG VALUE IT TRIGGERS

SQL> CREATE OR REPLACE TRIGGER C_TRI_INS
  2   BEFORE INSERT ON ST_INFO for each row
  3  BEGIN
  4    IF :NEW.SUB1<0 OR :NEW.SUB1>100 THEN
  5       RAISE_APPLICATION_ERROR(-20001,'MARKS NOT IN RANGE');
  6    END IF;
  7  END;
  8  /

Trigger created.
